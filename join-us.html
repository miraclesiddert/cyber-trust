OUR RECRUITMENT PROCESS

Applying for a new job is exciting but also daunting, especially if you don’t know what will happen next.

Our established recruitment process helps us assess your suitability for the role and enables you to find out all you need to know about us. 

Application received
Thank you! We will review your application and get back to you as soon as possible, normally within days, but certainly within 1-2 weeks.
Initial video call
A relaxed 15-20 minute online call where we ask a few set questions to learn more about your skills and career motivations. This is also an opportunity for you to ask any questions you might have about us, our culture and the role.
Virtual Assessment
You’ll be invited to complete an online assessment, but there’s no need to worry. It will help you to understand some of the tasks required of the role, and your results help us to assess your level of practical knowledge.
Final Interview
This will take place at one of our offices and usually with your direct line manager and members of the senior management team. We want to get to know you a little better and ask you further questions about your experience and aspirations. This is also a further opportunity to ask us anything you need to know about the company, our culture and the role.
Offer to join our #ONETEAM
Congratulations! If you successfully pass each stage of the process, we hope to make you an offer you’ll accept. Once the paperwork is complete, we’ll send you details of your onboarding process. Welcome to The DPO Centre and our #ONETEAM!

See our Candidate Recruitment Privacy Policy for full details of our data collection for vacancy applications.
